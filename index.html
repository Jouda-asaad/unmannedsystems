# about

We are a startup focused on Unmanned Aerial Systems (UAS). Our mission is to design and build dependable, efficient drone platforms and the software that powers them.

# tech

  • airframes Lightweight multirotor and fixed-wing platforms optimized for field performance.
  • autonomy Practical navigation, control, and safety systems with a focus on reliability.
  • payloads Modular sensor bays for inspection, mapping, and research applications.
  • ground Operator-friendly ground tools with a clean, fast, terminal-inspired UI.

# platforms

  • multirotor 40+ min flight time • swappable batteries • modular payload bay.
  • fixed‑wing Long‑range survey platform • VTOL option • efficient cruise profiles.

# use-cases

  • inspection Utilities, solar, wind — safe stand‑off imaging and reporting.
  • mapping Orthomosaic, DSM/DTM, corridor mapping with RTK/PPK options.
  • research Modular sensors and open interfaces for rapid experimentation.
